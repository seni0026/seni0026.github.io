About Me

My name is Kevon Senior. I recently left my home country, Jamaica, to study Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College as an International Student in Ottawa, ON. It has been very cold since i have arrived
here in Ottawa with temperature ranging from 35oF to -9oF. The sun seldomly shines. It is mostly overcast with rain, hail or snow falling. I am enjoying the winter season here in Ottawa. Seeing snow for the first
time was a wonderful experience for me mainly because i have never experience it before. I have yet to try out any of the winter activities such as ice skating, hockey, skiing, etc... I know i would enjoy them because
i am an adventurous person. I like trying new things, meeting different people and experiencing different cultures.

The program that i am enrolled in, Interactive Media Design, is a two (2) years diploma program. I chose this program because of my passion for digital graphics and all the other courses
it entails, such as; website development, photography, motion graphics, ui/ux design, etc.... Also, i was a part-time Graphic Designer in my home country. My dream is to be a Brand Identity
Designer and own a disign studio one day. So far, the program has been very interesting for me and i have no regrets comning to Algonquin College. This is my favorite quote that i constantly
remind myself of. It keeps me motivated to chase after my dreams and never give up;
